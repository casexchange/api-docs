## What's Inside

- **Authentication** - JWT session login, refresh, profile, password reset, and invitation-completion endpoints.
- **Case APIs** - Create, list, update, send, re-refer, manage status updates, and attach documents.
- **Directory APIs** - Firm directory, specialties, jurisdictions list, and firm detail endpoints.
- **Integrations & Routing** - Salesforce and Case Status integrations plus `POST /routing/evaluate-api`.
- **Error Handling** - Consistent response envelopes with shared error schemas across endpoints.
