## What’s Inside

- `Authentication` – Complete JWT and OAuth 2.0 flows plus refresh logic.
- `Case APIs` – Create, update, re-refer, or bulk manage referrals.
- `Directory APIs` – Discover firms, jurisdictions, and case types.
- `Routing` – Evaluate routing rules to automate firm suggestions.
- `Error Handling` – Consistent response schemas and rate-limit headers.
